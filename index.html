How to use:

Each point on the map represents a SNOTEL weather measuring station. SNOTEL is an automated system of snowpack and related climate sensors operated by the Natural Resources Conservation Service of the United States Department of Agriculture in the Western United States.

Click on a point to recieve the most recent SNOTEL reports from the station as well as forecasting information for that location.
